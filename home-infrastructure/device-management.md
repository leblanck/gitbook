All of our Apple devices are managed via Jamf (Jamf Now specifically)

### Log In

To access Jamf Now you can log in here:

### Policies

#### iOS

* Wi-Fi Profile
* Common Apps

#### macOS

* Lock Screen Messaging
* Wi-Fi Profile
* Enable FileVault
* 16 Character Password w/ 1 Year Max Age
